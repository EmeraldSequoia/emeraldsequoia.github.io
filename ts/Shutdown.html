Emerald Timestamp shutdown

As noted in our blog post here, Emerald Timestamp will be removed from the store November 1, 2023, and Emerald Sequoia LLC will be shutting down at the end of the year. Please see that blog link for details and motivations.

Note that this means you will potentially lose access to the events you have stored in the app. If you want to maintain a record of those events, you can email them to yourself in either text or CSV/TSV format:

  1. If you want to change the format from the default text to CSV or TSV:
    • From the primary screen, tap on Options at the bottom center, then tap on "Email format" to specify the format.
  2. Tap on any event on the primary screen to bring up the detail page.
  3. Tap on "Email all events".
  4. In the email window that pops up with your events in it, enter your email address and any other data you want to identify what you are sending.
  5. Tap the Send button (the blue up-arrow at the upper right in recent iOS versions) to send the email.
  6. Confirm that the email is received properly.

If you are unable to send email, ensure that Mail is set up properly on your device. If you can email from the iOS Mail app that's built into your device, you should be able to email using this mechanism.

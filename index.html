Unofficial status and repository for legacy apps and materials of Emerald Sequoia LLC

About

This is an unofficial archive containing materials from the original company Emerald Sequoia LLC, which is now shut down.

While maintained in part by one of the former co-founders (slpucci), this is not an official company site. Its purpose is to provide links both to relaunched versions and to archive materials for the original apps.
